About Us

The West Jordan Chamber of Commerce is a family of businesses that represents a strong voice in the local community. It is our goal to make the community stronger by supporting local businesses and creating a strong relationship between our members and the local community. We believe that when the community spends at home, the local businesses are able to offer greater support. Businesses are able to provide better prices on goods and provide more employment opportunities to the community.
