Site Name

West Jordan Chamber of Commerce

Domain

westjordancoc.com

Site Purpose

This chamber of commerce website will be used to promote networking and promotion of local businesses within the city limits. It will provide a business directory with information about each business that is a member of the chamber, and events related to the chamber business members. The primary goal of the West Jordan Chamber of Commerce is to bring spending back home to its local business members.
