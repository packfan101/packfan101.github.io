About the Artist

Amber Rausch is a passionate mother of five children and understands the difficulty of getting children ready along with herself each morning. She became excited about permanent cosmetics with the goal of helping others who have the same struggles as she does. She is a licensed and insured permanent cosmetic artist with a great attention to detail, and a devotion to her clients.

Microblading

Microblading is a tattooing technique in which each hair stroke is created by hand using a small handheld tool that utilizes tiny needles to make micro cuts in the skin. Microblading is used on the eyebrows to create, enhance and reshape their appearance in terms of both shape and color. This method differs from the sofTap procedure because it gives a more natural look of individual hair strokes in the eyebrow. This is a permanent procedure.

SofTap/Shade

The sofTap hand method of permanent makeup is a gentle way to implant permanent color into the skin. The sofTap needle configurations are designed to provide maximum comfort, fast healing, complete control, and little or no touchups. Like microblading, sofTap is used to shape and color your eyebrows but sofTap is more of a “full coverage” method. It is used to fill the complete area of the brow.

Permanent Eyeliner

Permanent eyeliner is a tattooing procedure right above the eyelash line to simulate eyeliner makeup. All eyeliner procedures are paired with a complimentary eyelash enhancement. The enhancement is used to fill in the lashes, creating a thicker, darker, and completely fuller look without the obvious look of heavy makeup.
