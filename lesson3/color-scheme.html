Color Scheme

I chose four colors for my site. They are as follows.

Color 1: rgb(132, 236, 111)

Light Green

I intend to use this as a background color on the page. It is easy on the eyes and black contrasts well with it.

Color 2: rgb(30, 17, 106)

Midnight Blue

This will be the color of my header and footer. It creates a strong contrast and helps to frame the page.

Color 3: rgb(239, 26, 136)

Magenta Process

This will be an accent color. I am using it as a background color for my main navigation section.

Color 4: rgb(255, 255, 255)

White

White will be my other accent color. I will use it primarily for text on dark backgrounds.
