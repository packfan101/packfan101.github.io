Target Audience

This website will target all the residents in the local community. This includes the residents of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. The site will benefit people who need the current weather conditions for the local area. It will also target residents that are interested in keeping up with local events and news. Most residents age ten and up have access to electronic devices that they can use to keep up with changes on the website. The website will be most popular with adults of all ages as they are typically the ones that organize schedules and make the types of trips that require up to date knowledge.

Weather changes quickly in Southeast Idaho, and residents are constantly checking weather conditions before making trips. Going outside for bicycling, camping, and hiking are common reasons residents look at the weather, but depending on the time of year residents will keep an eye on weather conditions when preparing to go shopping or take children to school.

Persona 1

Amy

Amy is an amazing mother of eight children. She is a stay at home mom who juggles the activities of all her kids on a daily basis. She is also highly active in social circles in her local community. She utilizes the benefits of this website on a daily basis. See below for some of the scenarios.

  • She checks the weather each morning to make sure her children are dressed aprropriately before school
  • She checks for storms to manage whether her animals need to be outside or in the barn.
  • She checks the weekly weather forcast before packing for family outings.
  • She checks the local news reports regularly to keep up on the happenings in the community.

Persona 2

Katie

Katie works outside on a daily basis. She counts on knowing the weather so that she can be prepared for her job. She deals with all types of weather from extreme heat causing frequent sunburn, to extreme cold an wet. Her shoes, pants and work shirts depend on the type of weather she will face that day. The need of protective gear such as gaiters for her legs, rain gear, and insulation depends on her knowledge of the weather. Katie is also a mother of three, and uses the weather when preparing for family outings and trips. See below for some scenarios of how she utilizes the website.

  • She checks the weather each morning while getting ready for work.
  • She checks the weather each evening to get the outlook for the next day.
  • She uses the weather to know what to pack when preparing for family outings.
  • She browses the news updates in the evenings after work to keep up on events.
