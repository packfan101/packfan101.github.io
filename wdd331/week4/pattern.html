Group 7 Pattern Library

Theme: Photography

Our group decided to go with a photography theme. We thought of the old polaroid cameras and how they had the instant self-developing film. We thought it would make an interesting page if we made the sections of the page look like polaroid photos displayed on a cork board. It is something you might see on the wall of a college dorm room.

Because it is kind of a retro theme with polaroid photos, we decided on using a stylish font for headlines that somewhat simulates a handwritten feel. The headings of each "photo" will be at the bottom like you would find if you wrote a comment on the bottom of a photo.
